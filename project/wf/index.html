For over 17 years Switch has been producing effective design solutions that are both unique and appropriate for small start-ups to established international brands in a variety of business sectors. Whether it be a brand launch, identity refresh or a targeted consumer experience, we approach every design problem with the same belief.

For us, design is not an insular process but a partnership and one that is built upon being open, collaborative and honest. Our successes have proven this approach to be a rewarding one, producing design that hasn’t simply window-dressed a solution but instead created something that’s become an intrinsic part of our clients’ businesses with lasting value.

From fashion and beauty to innovation and manufacturing, Switch is a generalist studio gathering insights and experience in business sectors that are constantly evolving. This has allowed us to create brand experiences in a variety of platforms and channels by persistently seeking out the most effective output for any given design challenge. By doing so, the brand remains central to our thinking and at the heart of everything we do.
